

Project name: Settlemint

A self-custodial Bitcoin cashflow engine that turns BTC-backed liquidity into automated monthly spending power.



What it does



Users deposit BTC into Mezo, mint or access MUSD, and let a vault manage their spending needs automatically. Instead of manually selling Bitcoin or constantly rebalancing funds, the app keeps capital productive and schedules recurring payouts for rent, bills, subscriptions, or creator payments.



The user connects with Mezo Passport, sets a spending plan, and chooses where the money should go. The vault then:



keeps the user’s BTC collateralized position healthy,

allocates MUSD into approved yield strategies,

forecasts upcoming cashflow needs,

and releases MUSD on schedule to merchants, billers, or the user’s wallet.

Why this is a strong Mezo project



This is a direct fit for Supernormal dApps - MUSD Track because it makes MUSD feel like normal money: invisible, scheduled, and useful in everyday life. It also overlaps with Bank on Bitcoin because it helps users save, borrow, and spend against BTC without selling it.



It also lines up well with the judging criteria:



Mezo integration: MUSD is the main spending and payout currency, and Mezo Passport handles identity/session control.

Business viability: recurring payments, budgeting, and automated spending are real consumer finance needs.

User experience: users only set the plan once; the system handles the rest.

Technical depth: vault logic, payout scheduling, risk thresholds, and strategy routing give you enough complexity for a serious demo.
